H.2 Effective Latency

Leff = Σ (hit_ratei × latencyi)

With 95% HBM hit rate, 4.5% CXL hit, 0.5% flash:

Leff = 0.95×100 + 0.045×250 + 0.005×25000 = 95 + 11.25 + 125 = 231 ns
